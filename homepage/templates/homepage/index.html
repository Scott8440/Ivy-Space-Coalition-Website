THE IVY SPACE COALITION

Who We Are

The Ivy Space Coalition consists of a group of undergraduate students across the Ivy League Universities who are passionate about aerospace engineering and have come together to help develop the next generation of entrepreneurs and aerospace engineers. In the Spring of 2019, we will be hosting our second annual conference at the University of Pennsylvania, once again seeking to connect students interested in space and aerospace engineering among the Ivy League Universities.

Join Us

If you are interested in attending the 2019 conference or would like to get involved with its planning, email Audrey Walsh (audwalsh@seas.upenn.edu).
