THE IVY SPACE COALITION

Who We Are

The Ivy Space Coalition consists of a group of undergraduate students across the Ivy League Universities who are passionate about aerospace engineering and have come together to help develop the next generation of entrepreneurs and aerospace engineers. In the Spring of next year, a conference will be held at Yale University, the first of its kind to connect students interested in aerospace engineering among the Ivy League Universities.
